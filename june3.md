## Dynamic Layout

<img src="https://cdn-media-1.freecodecamp.org/images/1*9nkJt3S1Fe_KMkDtpIhgXw.png" />

```html
<div class="container">

    <div class="row">
        <div class="col">
            <p class="text-center">This is a single column.</p>
        </div>
    </div>

    <div class="row">
        <div class="col">
            <p class="text-center">This is the first column.</p>
```
